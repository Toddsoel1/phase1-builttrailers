Warranty Registration

Register your trailer's warranty

Dealerships or trailer owners — register at the time of sale. Trailers must be registered within 15 days of the sale date by the selling dealership.

The warranty start date is the purchase date shown on this document. Built Trailers verifies the date against your upload before the warranty is active.

Submit a warranty claim

Trailer owners and selling dealerships can submit a claim. Built Trailers will follow up to schedule the repair.

Add photos or a short video so Built Trailers can validate the claim faster.

Keep your warranty in good standing by recording required maintenance as you complete it.
