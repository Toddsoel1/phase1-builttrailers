Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

Built Trailers ("we," "us," or "our") operates a shop management platform used internally by Built Trailers staff and, in limited ways, by our customers and dealer partners. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

SMS / Text Message Notifications

What we send. When you opt in, Built Trailers may send you text messages related to your orders or, for employees, approval request notifications. Message types include order status updates (Quote, Confirmed, In Production, Ready/Shipped) and purchase order or vendor approval requests.

Message frequency. Customers and dealers receive up to 4 messages per order — one per production stage. Employees receive one message per approval request assigned to them. Frequency varies based on order and approval activity.

Message and data rates may apply. Standard message and data rates charged by your mobile carrier may apply to messages sent to or received from Built Trailers.

Your mobile number will not be shared. We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your mobile phone number to any third party for marketing or promotional purposes. Your number is used solely to deliver the notifications you opted into and is never provided to advertisers or data brokers.

How to opt out. Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe. You will receive a one-time confirmation and no further messages will be sent. Reply START or UNSTOP at any time to re-subscribe.

Help. Reply HELP to any message or contact us at info@builttrailers.com for support.

How to opt in. You may opt in by texting START, YES, IN, or ENROLL to +1 (435) 900-8198, or by completing the opt-in form at builttrailers.app/optin. Consent is never required to make a purchase or to work with Built Trailers.

Information We Collect

We collect information you or Built Trailers staff provide when setting up accounts or placing orders, including:

  • Name and contact information (phone number, email)
  • Order details (trailer model, quantity, delivery date)
  • Account credentials (username; passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes — never in plain text)
  • SMS opt-in records including consent timestamp and opt-in method (text keyword or web form)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Manage trailer orders and notify you of production status changes
  • Route purchase order and vendor approval requests to the appropriate approvers
  • Maintain an audit trail of system actions for internal compliance
  • Integrate with QuickBooks Online for accounting and invoicing

We do not use your information for advertising or sell it to third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to operate our services:

  • Twilio — our SMS delivery provider. Your phone number is transmitted to Twilio solely to deliver messages you have consented to receive. Twilio's privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy.
  • Intuit / QuickBooks Online — for invoice and billing data. Applicable only to order and accounting records, not SMS data.
  • Render — our hosting provider. Server infrastructure only; no access to application data.

Your mobile phone number is never shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Retention

Order and customer records are retained for the life of the business relationship and as required for tax and accounting purposes. SMS opt-in and opt-out records are retained indefinitely as evidence of consent. You may request deletion of your personal information by contacting us below.

Security

We use industry-standard security practices including bcrypt password hashing, JWT-based session authentication, HTTPS in production, and role-based access controls. Credentials are stored only in server-side environment configuration and are never exposed in browser requests or API responses.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To make a request, contact us at info@builttrailers.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page reflects when the current version was published. Continued use of our services after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
